How to Delete Your Data

According to Meta Platform rules, we provide a way for users to request the deletion of their data. If you have interacted with NYC Scout and want your information removed, please follow these steps:

Option 1: In-App Message (Recommended)

Send the message "DELETE MY DATA" to NYC Scout in your Instagram DM. This will flag your User ID for automatic removal from our databases within 48 hours.

Option 2: Email Request

Contact us at privacy@nycscout.nyc. Please include your Instagram username so we can identify the correct data to delete. We will process your request within 5 business days and send a confirmation.

What Data is Deleted?

Upon receiving a deletion request, NYC Scout will permanently remove:

  • Your Meta User Identifier (PSID).
  • All conversation history and message logs.
  • Saved preferences and recommendation history.
  • Rate limit usage data.

Note: Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered. If you interact with the bot again, a new profile will be created.
