1. Compliance with Meta Platform Terms

NYC Scout operates as a third-party application on the Meta (Instagram) platform. We comply with the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies to ensure your data is handled securely and responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with NYC Scout via Instagram DMs, we collect the following information:

  • Meta User Identifier (PSID): An app-scoped ID used to identify your session.
  • Profile Information: Publicly available information such as your name or username.
  • Message Content: The text of your messages and queries to provide relevant recommendations.
  • Preferences: Locations or types of activities you express interest in.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Provide personalized NYC recommendations (restaurants, events, etc.).
  • Maintain rate limits and prevent service abuse.
  • Improve our AI response mapping using anonymized data.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties or use it for targeted advertising outside of the NYC Scout service.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

To provide AI-powered answers, we may share anonymized message content with Google Gemini AI. Your Meta User ID is not shared with these AI providers. We ensure all third-party partners adhere to strict confidentiality standards.

5. User Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data at any time. For specific instructions on how to remove your data from our systems, please visit our Data Deletion Instructions page.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or our data practices, contact us at privacy@nycscout.nyc or message us directly on Instagram.
